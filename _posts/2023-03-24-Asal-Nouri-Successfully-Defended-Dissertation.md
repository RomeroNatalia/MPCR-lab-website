Congratulations to [Dr. Asal Nouri][Asal] on defending her dissertation, "[*Mechanisms of Selective Attention in Working Memory, Modeled from Human's Alpha Band Oscillations*]()" concluding her final degree requirement. Asal will be awarded her Ph.D. in Complex Systems and Brain Sciences at Florida Atlantic Unictersity's Spring 2023 commencement ceremony in May.

Congratulations Dr. Nouri!
