Congratulations to my friend and colleague [Stephen Hoover][SH] for being the first student at FAU to earn a master's degree in Artificial Intelligence *along the way* during his Ph.D. program!

[Stephen][SH] is a Ph.D. candidate at the Center for Complex Systems and Brain Sciences working in the MPCR lab and he is an AI Safety Fellow at FAU's [Center for the Future Mind][cfm]. He will attend the Spring 2024 commencement ceremony for the College of Engineering and Computer Science.
