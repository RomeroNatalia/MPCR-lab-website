# Stationery and Letterhead

## Letterhead and Envelopes  

<blockquote>
Approved FAU letterhead, envelopes and business cards can be ordered through the Purchasing department.  
<br>
<br>
For electronic communications, Microsoft Word letterhead templates are available to faculty and staff (for large quantity orders please visit the Purchasing department website).  
<br>
<br>
Two styles of FAU letterhead are available as templates. Each style comes in two-color (FAU Blue/FAU Red) and one-color (black-and-white) versions.
<br>
<br>
If you will be printing your letterhead on a color inkjet or color laser printer, use the two-color version.
<br>
<br>
If you will be printing your letterhead on a black-only inkjet or laser printer, use the black-and-white version.  
<br>
<br>
